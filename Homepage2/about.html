Our Heritage

A History of Pushing Boundaries

Over a decade of clinical milestones, pioneering technologies, and unwavering dedication to patient health.

2016

The Foundation

Stackly was established with a singular vision: to dismantle the rigid, assembly-line nature of traditional hospitals and replace it with a tech-forward, high-empathy clinic network.

2019

Going Digital-First

Launched our integrated Patient Portal system alongside electronic health records sequencing, cutting administrative processing loops for our incoming patients by 70%.

2022

Robotic Integration

Global Recognition
